The Privacy Ethos.

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

1. Data Cultivation

At Verdant Vault, we collect only the necessary data to ensure your specimens reach you safely and thrive in your care. This includes botanical zone information, humidity preferences, and historical growth logs provided for care consultations.

We never sell your botanical data to third-party greenhouses or marketing labs. your collection is your private sanctuary.

2. Seed Encryption

All transaction details are protected via AES-256 botanical encryption. Fund security is our primary concern after plant health.

3. Right to Forget

Should you choose to leave the inner circle, you have the right to request a complete botanical data wipe. All historical order logs and care guides will be permanently shredded from our offline vault within 72 hours.
