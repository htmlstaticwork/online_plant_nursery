Frequently Asked by Collectors

Q

Can I skip a month?

Absolutely. Most of our collectors skip during winter months or when they travel. One-click skip in your dashboard. No hidden fees or re-activation penalties.

Q

How do you ship in winter?

We use 72-hour UniHeat packs and extra-thick isothermal padding for all subscription shipments during colder seasons. Every specimen is double-boxed to maintain an internal temperature of at least 15°C (60°F) during transit.
